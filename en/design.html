design Thiking

In Design Thinking we look for a problem and we give you your solution, based on surveys, background and information. This time we have seen a big problem in Arequipa and almost all of our country, is the high level of traffic congestion during peak hours, which affects the passer-by standing and the drivers themselves.

Probable causes:

• Excessive population growth, since this implies that there are more transport companies and they lead to a larger number of units.

• The absurd quantitative growth of vehicles in the city.

• The large number of public works in our city which are carried out in major avenues or streets which affect the transportation routes.

• The growth of shopping centers where the influx of vehicles is greater affecting public transport.

Design Thinking project approach

In this case we will focus on the most critical situation in terms of traffic and what causes it, in Arequipa a great cause is the increase in works since they are not completed and a new one is initiated, there are several examples only district level but regional. and proposing a series of steps through advice to decongest the works and thus decongest the traffic.

Define and empathize

In this stage we will focus more on the population involved in this problem, as it is that when different public works are carried out they affect vehicular transit and consequently to the population.
