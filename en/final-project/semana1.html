Choice of Digital Manufacturing Project.

Our project is to develop a laptop support which gives us comfort when doing work, from any environment and any condition since its supports or legs, allow us to adapt to the bed, sofa, Grass and others.

The innovative in this product introducing us to digital electronics will be the implementation of a cooler in the lower part for cooling the laptop. At the same time we will place a sequence of LEDs which will interlace colors depending on the sequence that we give.

This support will be powered directly from the laptop through a USB port, allowing the cooler to activate and if the place where the client is located is dark, the LEDs will create a more formidable color environment to work with.

Socioeconomic impact

The socio-economic impact will be many but in terms of the social will have a lot of attachment for young students and people who depend a lot on a computer either for work or relaxation. This support will help them not be sitting in the same place with their laptops, but they can go anywhere and set up their table so they can have a good base to support their laptop.
