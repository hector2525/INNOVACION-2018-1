Choice of Digital Manufacturing Project.

Our project is to develop a laptop support (image 1) which gives us comfort when doing work, from any environment and any condition since its supports or legs, allow us to adapt to the bed, sofa, Grass and others.

The innovative in this product introducing us to digital electronics will be the implementation of a cooler in the lower part for cooling (image 2) the laptop. At the same time we will place a sequence of LEDsvwhich will interlace colors depending on the sequence that we give.

This support will be powered directly from the laptop through a USB (image 3) port, allowing the cooler to activate and if the place where the client is located is dark, the LEDs will create a more formidable color environment to work with.

Socioeconomic impact

The socio-economic impact will be many, but as for the social will have a lot of attachment for young students and people who depend a lot on a computer either for work or relaxation. This support will help you not to be sitting in the same place with your laptops, but to go anywhere and set up your table so that you can have a good base to support your laptop.

As for the economic impact, these supports regularly have a value of 25 to 30 soles in terms of only wood as a frame, and a cooler costs 30 to 35 soles. This is why several of the young students in various universities, institutes and colleges. You can not use both at the same time and we decided to put them together to give you the comfort that many want and at a much more affordable cost, offering a quality product and a wide sequence of colored lights with the RGB LEDs..

Implementation of electronics to the project.

Well, since our project must carry several electronic components we decided to detail them so that we can specify the steps we will take in these, the first point is the cooler that can be powered with the USB port of the laptop, in turn it could be connected to some outlet; this is how we can move it to the place we want without worrying about an outlet.

Now in terms of the sequence of RGB lights we can do it with the help of the Arduino program and at the same time we can control the 3 colors with power-ups, thus having the color that you like most just by playing with these power-ups.
