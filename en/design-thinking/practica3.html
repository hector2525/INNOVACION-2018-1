Here a series of encustas was made to residents and transporters who are being affected in various parts of the city with the problem of chaos, this is how knowing the opinion and suggestions of the population we can get to work together with the municipality and not against , affecting themselves.

We begin by having a series of questions which will help us to have and learn more about the thoughts and contributions of the people regarding the vehicle management in Arequipa, and later to know according to them what are the points or causes for which the traffic in Arequipa is So overwhelming and how to solve it, after that we will propose our proposal by making known one of the solutions to traffic in Arequipa.

These questions are:

How do you consider the vehicular traffic in Arequipa?

What problems do you think this congestion causes?

Are you satisfied with the work done by the municipalities to reduce this traffic?

As it could be observed, 3 people involved in what would be vehicular management, a traffic policeman, were interviewed, they try to eliminate this congestion and bring order to the vehicles, being an arduous job, an owner of a driving license company , being one of the people who instruct the drivers, and in charge of teaching them everything about the vehicular management, and finally a taxi driver, as we know they are workers who are very involved in the traffic and at the same time harmed, and knowing the opinion of the 3 can make a more detailed analysis of their needs.
