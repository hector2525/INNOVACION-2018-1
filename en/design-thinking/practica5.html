Prototype

In this stage we will focus more on the product to deliver, in this case we will deliver an informative magazine, as we compare with the magazine ARGUMENTS, in this case this magazine will be distributed more than anything in Arequipa, and later in the future can see the vehicular chaos in Arequipa and you can see the solution.

SPECTATOR Magazine

In which our magazine consists. Our magazine consists in informing the Arequipeño people of the great chaos that we have today, and the various factors that involve in vehicular congestion, is how we propose that people, upon learning about these methods, could improve our daily life and make it better, both drivers like pedestrians.

This magazine will be attached to the APA format special for magazines, and so after printing copies and distribute to all public, our varied solutions consist in raising awareness to readers, giving them guidelines to avoid a big headache today, giving them useful instructions such as diversion plans, maps of Arequipa with a distribution of roads which could favor them when being in the middle of traffic.

Nowadays, finding a good deviation plan or even better a detour that saves us time and money is very feasible. Not only in the transport sector, this would involve all sectors and the population, of course these deviations will not always be for all types of vehicles, it depends a lot on vehicle, load, weight, etc. and type of use.
