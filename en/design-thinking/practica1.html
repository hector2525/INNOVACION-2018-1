Approach of the Design Thinking Project

In this opportunity using the Design Thinking tool we have decided to propose a solution based on a problem in the city of Arequipa, all this is through a series of stages such as surveys, information search, etc. All this just to give the comfort of the residents who are involved with this problem.

Introduction to Design Thinking

As the main objective of this project we have to analyze and propose a solution to the set of works that is carried out in simultaneous since so many works executing have several consequences as traffic, stress on drivers and passers-by, etc. That is why we planted a plan to be able to execute in an orderly manner all the works that are necessary but without affecting the town.
