Causes of vehicular congestion

According to Thomson (2002), the causes of vehicular congestion are varied. However, among the factors that cause it are short and long-term factors.

- Rapid population growth.

                    

- The intensive use of motor vehicles.

                    

- Excessive growth of the private car park.

                    

- Agglomeration of public works in our city without a due deviation plan.

                    

- Deficiency in the construction of road infrastructure.

The direct approach that we want to give to our project is to be able to contribute to the citizenship with the due information that is in our city so that we can maintain the public with the greatest possible knowledge of the problems that our city brings.
